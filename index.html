About

A quiet record of study

I am interested in the habits of careful learning: reading closely, writing clearly, and returning to difficult ideas until they become concrete. In mathematics, that often means tracing structure through definitions, proofs, and examples. In computer science, it means understanding how abstraction meets implementation.

The aim of this site is not to present finished expertise, but to document serious effort. Some entries are polished notes; others are provisional sketches, questions, or early intuitions worth revisiting. Over time, I hope the collection becomes both a personal reference and an honest map of what I have been learning.
